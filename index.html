A Historic Reunion: The Inaugural Alumni Meet

Cyber Vidhi Sangam marks the inaugural alumni meet for the esteemed Post Graduate Diploma in Cyber Law & Cyber Forensics (PGDCLCF) from NLSIU, PACE. Spearheaded by the batch of 2025, this event is more than just a conference; it's a homecoming. It's a unique opportunity for us, the alumni, to reconnect, share our journeys, and strengthen the bonds forged during our time at India's premier law school.

The core of the day is dedicated to connection and gratitude. We will host a special felicitation ceremony to honor the professors whose guidance has been instrumental in our careers. Following this, a dedicated meet-and-greet session will provide the perfect platform to build a robust professional network among peers who are now leading experts in the techno-legal domain across various industries.

Our Vision for the Future

While this meet celebrates our shared past, its gaze is fixed firmly on the future. Cyber Vidhi Sangam is the cornerstone of a much larger ambition. In a nation rapidly digitizing, the need for a unified, authoritative techno-legal voice has never been more critical.

This event is the first step in transforming our powerful alumni network into a formidable national techno-legal forum. Our vision is to create a permanent platform for policy advocacy, industry collaboration, and continuous learning that can guide India through the complex challenges at the intersection of technology and law.
