About The Conference

Exploring the Frontiers of Cyber Law and Digital Forensics

Cyber Bidhi Sangam 2025 is a premier gathering of legal professionals, cybersecurity experts, law enforcement officials, and academics. Our mission is to foster a dialogue on the evolving landscape of cyber law, addressing critical issues like data privacy, digital evidence, AI regulation, and the challenges posed by emerging technologies. This conference serves as a confluence of ideas, aiming to shape the future of digital jurisprudence and forensic practices in India and beyond.
